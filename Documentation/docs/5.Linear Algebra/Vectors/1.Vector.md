## Vector Operations

<br>

### Vector Addition

$$
\left[\begin{array}{cc}
-1 \\
2
\end{array}\right]
+
\left[\begin{array}{cc}
3 \\
 4
\end{array}\right]
=
\left[\begin{array}{cc}
2 \\
-6
\end{array}\right]
$$

<hr />

### Vector Substraction

$$
\left[\begin{array}{cc}
-1 \\
2
\end{array}\right]
-
\left[\begin{array}{cc}
3 \\
 4
\end{array}\right]
=
\left[\begin{array}{cc}
-4 \\
-2
\end{array}\right]
$$

<hr />

### Vector Multiplication

<div style="border: 1px solid black; border-radius:20px;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;">
$$
\begin{align}
\overrightarrow{A} = (-1,2)\\
\overrightarrow{B} = (3,4) \\
\overrightarrow{A} \cdot \overrightarrow{B} = (-1)(3)+(2)(4) \\
= -3 + 8 \\
= 5
\end{align}
$$
</div>

<div style="border: 1px solid black; border-radius:20px;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;">
$$
\begin{align}
AB=
\left[\begin{array}{cc}
-1 & 2
\end{array}\right]
\cdot
\left[\begin{array}{cc}
3 \\
4\\
\end{array}\right]
= (-1)(3)+(2)(4) \\
= -3 + 8 \\
= 5
\end{align}
$$
</div>
